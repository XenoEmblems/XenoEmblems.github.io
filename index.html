About Me

Trevaughn Bynum is the consummate artist. She is multi-disciplined with a range that spans from fine art to design to writing and directing. Trevaughn studied Scenic Art and Stage Properties at The University of North Carolina School of the Arts.


After doing theater for several years she undertook a self-imposed challenge to write a full length play. It started with a poem on a post it note and eventually became a full-length spoken work play she named "Waitin'".
